About the Band

The Story

"We make rock music and spend way too much time making videos for all our songs."

Mr. GnG is a two-piece rock band born from an unlikely pandemic reunion. Ishan Dave (India) and Abhilash Sreekumar (France) were college friends who reconnected after six years apart and realised they had unfinished musical business.

Recording and producing remotely across continents, the duo crafted their debut EP EPonymous — five songs, five music videos — dropping it on Christmas Day 2022. The record moves through themes of farewell, longing, hope, and raw catharsis, with each track paired with a dedicated visual.

In 2025, they returned with after Us, The Flood — a full-length album and a significant leap forward. Twelve tracks exploring self-doubt, abandonment, and existential vulnerability, featuring guest appearances from LA guitarist Rudy Ayoub and guitarist Aatrey Bhatt.

For mrgng, the sonic and the visual are inseparable. Everything is self-produced, self-directed, and uncompromising — from the studio to the stage.

The live line up consists of session players based in Ahmedabad.

Members

Ishan Dave
Vocals · Guitar · Producer
Based in India. Writes and records from home. Fronts mrgng with a guitar sound that swings between cathartic and carefully restrained — and directs the band's visual output.
Abhilash Sreekumar
Keys · Drums · Producer
Based in France. Handles the rhythmic and harmonic architecture across thousands of miles — late nights and a laptop producing every beat and chord on the EP.
Vaastu
Lead Guitar · Live
Ahmedabad's hottest new lead guitar rockstar, known for his session and live work for dozens of well known local outfits and musical acts. Newest member of the live line up, Vaastu wrote the solo for R.A.Z.E.D and features in the video. Joined in January 2026.
Meet Gadhia
Drums · Live
One of the oldest members of the live lineup, Meet has been playing with the band since 2022. Known for his groovy playing and incessant practice routines, Meet is the go-to drummer for many well known artists in Gujarat.
Rohan Shah
Keys · Live
Keyboardist by night and a businessman by day, Rohan is the youngest member of the family. Always ready for rehearsals and probably the most energetic member of the band.
Jagdis Jethwa
Live Musician
Joined right before the comeback show at Rhaen Basra in April 2026. The most diligent and hard working member of the band, Jagdis plays live and in the studio with the likes of Aditya Gadhavi. Ishan met him when they played together for Kinjal Dave in November 2024.
